About Me

I'm from Berkeley, CA, but I grew up in Bethesda, MD, moving halfway through highschool. I'm so glad I moved–I love the west coast (the weather and hiking) and the opportunities I had here during the last half of high school, especially playing and touring with the Berkeley High jazz ensemble.

Why Dali?

I can stay focused on a coding project for hours straight, drawn along by the satisfaction of solving one small problem after another. I love the feeling of building something useful and usable, and the DALI lab would provide me constant opportunities to do so. Applying skills I’ve learned in classes is in itself a skill, one which I hope to gain through DALI. I also hope to meet more people who, like me, are interested in CS, as I’ve found that people who share my interests are the ones most fun to befriend.
